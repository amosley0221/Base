Install app

Browser

Add BASE to your home screen for a full-screen, app-like experience that works offline. Your data lives on your device and stays put across app updates — updating the app never wipes what you've entered.

On iPhone / iPad: tap the Share icon in Safari, then Add to Home Screen. On Android, open the browser menu and tap Install app / Add to Home screen.

Backup & restore

keep a copy safe

Download a backup file to your phone or cloud drive. If you ever switch devices, clear your browser, or want to be extra safe, restore from it here. Backups include everything — tasks, habits, notes, calendar, school, budget and teams.

Restore by pasting text instead

Export to PDF

pick what to include

Choose the sections you want and open a clean, print-ready view. From there, use your browser's Print → Save as PDF.

Reset

Restore the original sample data, or wipe everything to a blank slate. Consider downloading a backup first.
